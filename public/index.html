ℹ️ About PrithviNet

Smart Environmental Monitoring Platform for Chhattisgarh

🎯 Our Mission

PrithviNet is a smart environmental monitoring platform built to safeguard Chhattisgarh's air, water, and acoustic environment. We provide real-time intelligence across four key cities — Raipur, Bilaspur, Bhilai, and Baloda Bazar — empowering both government operators and everyday citizens to make informed decisions about their environment.

🌬️ Air Quality Tracking — PM2.5, PM10, NO₂, SO₂, CO, O₃ with AQI classification
💧 Water Health Monitoring — pH, dissolved oxygen, BOD, and turbidity analysis
🔊 Noise Pollution Mapping — Zone-wise ambient noise tracking against CPCB standards
📍 Citizen Awareness — Live location-based dashboards for neighbourhood monitoring
